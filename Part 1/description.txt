This program is designed to simulate simple accountant operations of a businesses that bid for projects. 
The program should house a list of Clients that have at least one budget active, with the option to hold more than one budget for each client.
Every budget should have at least an estimate, name, and project description, with the potential to hold a list of expense and payment information.
Each budget should be able to give a total of expenses and payments, as well as the estimate vs expense, and profit for the project.
Program should print two reports: a client list and a budget report for each client.
